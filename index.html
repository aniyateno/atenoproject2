Bio

Aniya Teno is a R&B and Neo Soul artist based in New Orleans, LA. She has been releasing music since 2020 and is a rising star in the R&B world. She is currently working on a project with her friend and partner 7 (Deven Raval).

Events

Aniya does a lot of shows in the New Orleans area where she is based. She has done Freret Street Festival, Gasa Gasa, Tiny Den, and an ongoing series called the Detour that she headlines frequently.
